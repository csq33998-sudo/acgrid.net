AllChinaBuy QC Photo Review Guide

AllChinaBuy spreadsheet shoppers usually compare QC photos before opening a product page. This guide explains what to check when browsing streetwear finds through MaisonLooks category links.

What to Check in QC Photos

Start with product shape, color, stitching, print placement, hardware, and material texture. For shoes, compare toe shape and panel spacing. For hoodies and jackets, check zipper alignment, embroidery, labels, and sleeve proportions.

How Spreadsheet Pages Help

A clean AllChinaBuy spreadsheet page reduces search time. Instead of scanning unrelated listings, shoppers can move through categories, compare notes, and use QC photo keywords before deciding which MaisonLooks product page to open.

Best Categories to Review First

Shoes, hoodies, jackets, bags, and accessories usually benefit most from QC photo review because small construction details can change how the item looks in an outfit.
